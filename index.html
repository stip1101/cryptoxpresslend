AMBASSADOR PROGRAM

The CryptoXpress Ambassador Program is designed to reward active content creators and community builders. Ambassadors will be evaluated weekly based on their performance in community activity, content creation, and engagement.

Ambassadors Roles

CryptoXpress operates a structured three-level ambassador program to ensure active engagement, reward contributions, and provide clear growth opportunities. Each level comes with increasing responsibilities, expectations, and benefits, allowing ambassadors to specialize in different areas such as content creation, community engagement, and moderation.

Initiate Level 1

The Initiate role is the starting point for every ambassador who joins the CryptoXpress Ambassador Program.

Requirements

  • Complete the Intro Tasks in Telegram app
  • Complete at least 5 tasks from Content Creation

Benefits

  • Full participation in the ambassador program
  • Access to exclusive ambassadors-only chat
  • New engagement opportunities

To Keep The Role

  • Earn 100 points in Community category
  • Collect 50 points per week in Content Creation
  • Collect 30 points per week in Engagement

Pioneer Level 2

Ambassadors who demonstrate consistent participation, high-quality task execution, and strong community engagement are promoted to Pioneer.

Requirements

  • Complete tasks in Content Creation, Community, and Engagement
  • Earn at least 3,200 points to unlock Level 2

Benefits

  • Access to paid tasks in Weekly Rewards
  • Unique, personalized achievements on profile

To Keep The Role

  • Earn 200 points in Community category
  • Collect 100 points per week in Content Creation
  • Collect 50 points per week in Engagement

Architect Level 3

Pulse ambassadors represent the top tier of the CryptoXpress Ambassador Program.

Requirements

  • Complete tasks in all categories including Weekly Rewards
  • Earn at least 6,500 points to unlock Level 3

Benefits

  • Eligibility for monthly rewards
  • Exclusive, limited-edition personalized achievement

To Keep The Role

  • Earn 250 points in Community category
  • Collect 100 points per week in Content Creation
  • Collect 50 points per week in Engagement

Progression System

Promotions

Evaluations occur on a 4-week cycle based on performance and contribution levels.

Ambassadors who meet KPIs and accumulate the required points within 1-2 months are eligible for promotion.

Achievements & Specialization

In addition to role-based progression, ambassadors can earn specialized recognition based on their strongest contributions (e.g., Content Creator, Community Leader, Event Organizer).

Demotions

Ambassadors who fail to meet their role requirements for two consecutive weeks risk demotion or removal from the program.

Rewards System

All ambassadors starting from Level 2 are eligible to receive rewards for their contributions to the ambassador program. The higher your level, the greater the opportunities for financial incentives and exclusive perks.

Level 2 Pioneer: Weekly Rewards

Ambassadors who achieve Level 2 gain access to the Weekly Rewards System, where they can complete predefined, rewarded tasks that reset every week.

  • The top-performing ambassador in the Weekly Rewards System accumulates points, which are converted into a financial reward at the end of the month.
  • Important: Level 2 ambassadors do not qualify for fixed monthly financial compensation but can maximize their earnings through performance-based incentives.

Level 3 Architect: Monthly Financial Rewards

Upon reaching Level 3, ambassadors unlock access to monthly financial rewards, structured to reflect their contributions to the program.

  • Compensation is distributed proportionally, meaning individual earnings are based on an ambassador's contribution relative to the total ambassador pool.
  • Higher engagement and impactful contributions result in a larger share of the financial reward pool.
